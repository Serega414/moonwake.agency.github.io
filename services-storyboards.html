EXAMPLE of the storyboad
A storyboard is a graphic representation of how your video will unfold, shot by shot. Like a script, your storyboard visually guides you throughout the production process.
Moonwake Agency make a lot of storyboards type: Shooting boards, stilomatics, animatics. These storyboards are used to turn your thoughts into drawings.

We use them in the advertisement, movies, motion design, games, education and many other industries.

It allows our clients to imagine final result before it's done.
