Animated Marketing: 5 WHYs
It Helps To HUMANIZE Your Brand
People always respond to people – that's so simple. Steer away from the robotic marketing tactics and instead speak the same language as your audience, focus on being interesting and most importantly, Human. Animated video marketing is the most powerful way to introduce your brand in the digital age. And without doubt, motion picture catches consumers' attention and inspires viewers to engage to what your brand is talking about.
It Makes People Captivated By Your IDEAS
Yes, we know that it's impossible for businesses to differentiate themselves without a brand. It is critical for companies that are focused on succeeding in the digital space. Every prominent brand has something that drives it, something special that sets it aside – a basis so to speak. The core idea, let's put it this way. What could be better than an idea embodied in an animation video? The idea which will excite the audience without feeling like it's already been done before. In particular, explainer videos are used to educate customers about how to use your product, hire new staff, and for dozens of other corporate and marketing purposes.
It SELLS Without Being Sales
People are always happy to buy when they find something that meets their needs, but they do not like being sold to. Imagine when you're in a store and a salesperson asks if you need anything? How does it feel? Your defense mechanisms go up. You know they do! And you still think you're willing to do whatever it takes to make the sale. But are you sure it's worth it? This opens the door for an animated video to take care of you how to do just that, sell without being too salesy! Videos that feature what you're selling and encourage customers to buy.
It Affects Google Ranking And Improves SEO
Google's algorithms are more-and-more prioritizing websites with authentic video content. Placing an animated(explainer or sales) video on the top of your homepage or landing page helps your website to rank on top in search engines which result in organic search results.
It Is Easy Shareable
Viewers love to share engaging content with their friends and relatives, whatever it is, advertising, music video, or creative cartoon. Creative videos are exceedingly easy to share across social media. It will fastly boost brand awareness. Place an animated video about your brand, and who knows, maybe it will become the next overnight sensation due to the original style and captivating story.
